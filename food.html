Anni's Pancake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 Banana
  • 100 g Flour
  • 1 tsp Baking Soda
  • A pinch of Salt
  • 100 ml plantbased Milk
  • Oil for cooking
  • Maple Syrup for serving

Instructions

  1. Peel the banana, place in a bowl and mash with a fork until mashed.
  2. Mix the flour, baking powder, sugar and salt in a bowl.
  3. Add the plant-based milk and banana puree and mix to form a thick batter. Leave the dough to rest for approx. 10 minutes.
  4. Heat some vegetable oil in a non-stick frying pan over a medium heat and fry the pancakes one after the other. As soon as small bubbles form, turn the pancakes over so that they are golden brown on both sides.
  5. Serve the pancakes with a drizzle of Maple Syrup.
